Hello. Unfortunately as of yesterday, Google Chrome has decided that it will no longer support Silverlight in it’s newest version.  Once you update to the latest version of Chrome, our dashboard will no longer function.  Here are two options to continue to view your data:
  • Use Internet Explorer 9.0 or greater to view the dashboard.
  • Enable NPAPI support to view your dashboard data in Chrome:
    1. Enter into your Chrome address bar:  chrome://flags/#enable-npapi
    2. Click Enable
    3. Close and reopen your browser
If the 'Enable NPAPI' is not an option, you will need to update to the latest version of Google Chrome.  Click here for instructions
 

We still encourage you to use Google Chrome for the (OI) operator interface.  We are in the process of completing the new dashboard that will include advanced reporting, speed enhancements and support for most devices and browsers.  Due to this unfortunate issue with Google Chrome, we will be releasing it early with the graphs you are familiar with.  The new graphs that include Shift, Efficiency and SKU information will be released at a later date. 

Have a great week! If you need anything please let us know at Support@ThriveMES.com.
